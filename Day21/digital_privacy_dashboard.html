Privacy improvement plan
1
Audit Google account data
Visit myaccount.google.com → Data & Privacy. Review Web & App Activity, Location History, YouTube History. Pause what you don't need.
2
Restrict Instagram + Facebook ad preferences
Meta's "Ad Preferences" → turn off activity-based targeting, off-Facebook activity data sharing.
3
Review TikTok data and permissions
TikTok Privacy Center → request your data, revoke location and contacts access in phone settings.
4
Enable Apple's "App Tracking Transparency"
Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Deny cross-app tracking. Reduces Meta + third-party SDK tracking significantly.
5
Use separate email for shopping apps
Prevents Amazon + Meesho from linking to your Google identity and social profiles.
6
Download and review your data from each platform
Most platforms support GDPR/DPDP data download requests. Knowing what's held is the first step to managing it.
