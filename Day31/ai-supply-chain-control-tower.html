How to Play

You're the Head of Operations. Alerts will appear describing real supply-chain problems. Each one has a priority and a countdown — respond before it expires or it resolves itself, badly.

Priorities

  • Critical — major revenue and service risk, act fast.
  • High — significant impact, needs attention soon.
  • Medium — manageable, but ignoring it adds up.

Actions

Each alert offers a few response options. The best option depends on the situation — some cost more but protect service and revenue, others are cheap but risky. "Ignore" and "Delay" are always available but usually cost you.

Scoring

Good decisions raise your KPIs and score. Bad ones raise operating cost and hurt customer satisfaction. Some consequences arrive a few seconds later, so watch the log.
